Attendance

Attendance at Lane Tech is taken every period. Unexcused absences will result in demerits, which are mostly used as an incentive by allowing or dissalowing acess to school events such as Homecoming or Prom. Absences can be excused with a form which is available in the main office, or having an adult use Parent Portal or call the attendance office (before 9:30 A.M.)
