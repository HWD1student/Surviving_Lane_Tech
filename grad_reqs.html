Graduation Requirements

English 4 Mathematics 4 Science 4
Social Science 3 World Language 3 Fine Arts* 2
Computer Science 1 Physical Education 2** College Prep Electives*** 5
  • *Fine arts - art, drama, music, etc.
  • **Students must complete 4 years of PE or a PE waiver form
  • ***All electives at Lane are college prep electives

Lane Tech has different requirements from the rest of CPS. The table on the left is the graduation requirements for the core concentration. Other concentrations have different graduation requirments, which usually involve taking four years of the subject and completing a project at the end of senior year. Students must also complete a civics course (which will be integrated into a social science class) and at least one AP class. Service learning hours are no longer required for graduation, but each student must complete 2 service learning projects, one of which must be part of a civics/AP government course.
