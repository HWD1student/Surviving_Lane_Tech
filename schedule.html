Introduction

Welcome To Lane Tech! Chances are you don't actually know all that much about the school and how things work here, so this website is designed to introduce you and teach you a few things that no one tells you before hand. Think of this as a supplemental to the website and orientation. Happy reading!

Schedule

Lane Tech has two main bell schedules that it uses when there are no special occasions or events. There are some situations where alterante bell schedules may be used, such as for assemblies, finals, or pep rallies, but these are rarely used and change frequently. The bells may also be silenced for various reasons. The regular bell schedule, which is used on all weekdays except for Wednesdays, has eight periods and runs from 8:00 A.M. to 3:15 P.M., with 50-minute periods and 5-minute passing periods. The advisory bell schedule, which is used on Wednesdays, has 9 periods, including Advisory, which comes after 1st period. Advisory, which is used as a study hall, social-emotional learning, or college readiness period (depending on the week) is a 40-minute period. All regular periods are 42 minutes, and passing periods are 5 minutes. The school day begins at 8:00 A.M. and ends at 2:56 P.M.. Your advisory is also your homeroom and you will usually also meet for events such as PSAT testing and assemblies. Your sechedule (regular bells) is listed on your ID, as well as your lunch period. Your lunch period can be 3rd-6th period and you will (usually) be allowed to go outside during the second semester your fresman year and the entire year for all subsequent years.
