Navigating Aspen

Lane Tech, Like many other schools, uses Aspen to assign grades. To check your grades, simply select the "Academics" tab at the top left corner of the page. However, Aspen contains much more information that you can access. For example, you can click on a class to access more details about it, including your attendance, grades in different categories and assignments, and curriculum maps if they exist. You can also see your information under the "My Info" tab, including your schedule and transcript. You can also set email notifications to alert you when attendance or grades are entered.
