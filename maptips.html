Navigating Lane Tech

At first glance, Lane Tech's map and room numbering system may seem complicated and arbitrary. However, Navigating Lane Tech becomes much easier with more undertanding of the room numbering system and layout. All of Lane's even-numbered rooms are located on the west (left on the map) side of the building, and all of the odd-numbered rooms are located on the east (right on the map) side of the building. Additionally, smaller-numbered rooms tend to be on the north side of the building, and larger-numbered rooms tend to be on the south side. The first digit of a room number also indicates what floor it is on; for example room 123 is located on the first floor, whereas room 431 is located on the 4th floor. There are four main hallways, which connect doors/ stairwells O and H, A and M, D and J, and A and D. Doors A, D, J, and M are the main enterances which students arrive through (A and D to the north, M and J to the south), of which M is the most important and most often open. If you want to know the general location of a room, you can also use the "Find A Room" button in this webpage.
